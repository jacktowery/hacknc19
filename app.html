About VENT

What is VENT?

VENT searches for concerts, sporting events, and more using your GPS location and the radius you specified to search. If you see an event you're interested in, swipe right to save it, or swipe left if you're not interested. Tap the    icon, and VENT will email you the list of events you are interested in. It's that simple!

How Was VENT Created?

VENT was created in just 24 hours for HackNC 2019, a Hackathon at UNC Chapel Hill.

Tools used: Bulma, Ticketmaster Discovery API, EmailJS
